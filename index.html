Press ⌘ Space, type Terminal, and press Return.

Questions

Is this safe? Why Terminal?

Terminal is Apple's built-in tool for running an installer. Pasting the one line is actually the safest, simplest path — it avoids the “unidentified developer” warnings you'd hit with a downloaded file. The installer only sets up free, open-source tools and asks before anything big.

How long does it take?

Usually 5–15 minutes, mostly downloading. It's fine to leave running — it tells you when it's finished with a checklist.

What if Right ⌘ does nothing at first?

Finish the two switches from step 3, then click the microphone icon in your top menu bar and choose Reload Config.

How do I remove it?

Quit Hammerspoon and drag it to the Trash. The voice files live in a folder called whisper.cpp and one called .local-whisper in your home folder — delete those and it's gone.
